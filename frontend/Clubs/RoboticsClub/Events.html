Internal Hackathon For Smart India Hackathon 2023

 21st September 2023. N.B. Hall

• The objective of the Internal Hackathon for Smart India Hackathon 2023 is to bring together talented individuals to collaboratively develop innovative solutions addressing challenges faced by India.

• Participants will leverage their skills in technology and problem-solving to create impactful projects, contributing to the country's progress.

• The event aims to foster creativity, collaboration, and technological advancements for the benefit of various sectors in India.
